Pita bread pizza recipe

An easy to make pita bread pizza. These are the ingredients you need and the steps you have to take to make the pizza!

Ingredients:

  • Pita bread or breads
  • 1 onion
  • Tomato sauce
  • Whatever meat you want, I used a tuna for this pizza
  • cheese

Steps:

  1. Slice the onion
  2. Add tomato sauce on the pita bread
  3. Add cheese on the pita bread
  4. Add onions on the pita bread
  5. Add tuna on the pita bread
  6. Put the bread on the oven (17 minutes, 240 degree)
