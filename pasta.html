Pasta recipe

An easy to make pasta. These are the ingredients you need and the steps you have to take to make the pasta!

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • 1 onion
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Cheese
  • Salt

Steps:

  1. Slice the onion
  2. Cook the pasta
  3. Put onion into the frying pan
  4. Put ground beef into the frying pan
  5. Add salt and tomato sauce and mix it
  6. Cook for 5-10 minutes
  7. Add cheese to the pasta
