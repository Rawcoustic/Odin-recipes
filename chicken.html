Chicken curry recipe

An easy to make chicken curry. These are the ingredients you need and the steps you have to take to make the chicken curry!

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Creme fraiche
  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Curry powder

Steps:

  1. Slice the onion, chicken and garlic
  2. Cook the the rice
  3. Put onion and garlic into the frying pan
  4. Put chicken into the frying pan
  5. Add salt, curry powder and creme fraiche and mix it
  6. Cook for 5-10 minutes
